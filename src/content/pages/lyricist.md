Lyrics bridge his love for rhythm, faith, and people. Whether writing a Shiv Stuti, a playful Holi song, or an urban Bengali melody, he focuses on sincerity over ornamentation. Each lyric invites singers and listeners into a shared space of devotion or reflection.
